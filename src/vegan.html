Why be vegan?
  • Animals are poorly treated, and then they're killed.
  • On a global basis, animal farming is a major contributor to climate change. Though admittedly, that's only because so many people aren't driving cars yet.
Is it safe to be vegan?
  • Yes, a diet can be nutritionally adequate with or without consuming meat and other animal products.
